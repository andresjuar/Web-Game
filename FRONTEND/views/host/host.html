What is Liar Game?

One player is the Subject — they answer a personal question about themselves honestly. Everyone else are Liars who must write a convincing fake answer.

The goal? Fool the group into thinking your lie is the truth — or spot who's lying!

🎯
The Two Roles
🎯

Subject: You receive a personal question and answer it honestly. Your goal is to have the most people vote for your real answer.

🎭

Liar: You receive the same question and write a convincing fake answer. The more people you fool, the more points you earn!

🗳️
How Voting Works

All answers are shown anonymously. Players vote for the answer they think is TRUE.

The Subject can't vote. Everyone else casts one vote, then the reveal shows who was who — and the points flow!

⭐
How to Earn Points
🎯 Subject: voted correct → +300 pts each 🎭 Liar: fooling a voter → +500 pts each 🗳️ Voter: correct guess → +200 pts

Write creatively, vote carefully, and bluff like a pro. The best liar wins! 🏆
